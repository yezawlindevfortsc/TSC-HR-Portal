03 / WHAT TO ADD

Modules still to build

Fifteen modules close the gaps, grouped by priority. Tier 1 is the core operational backbone with the widest task coverage; tiers 2–3 add cycles, enabling functions and governance.

Tier 1 · core operationshighest task coverage · Phase 2

1 · Payroll & Cost Control

Payroll · Manager

Monthly payroll, CPF / IR21 / IR8A, reconciliation under maker-checker, leave encashment, bond-break, manpower cost reports by cost centre, wage-subsidy reconciliation. Deterministic calculation engine — the money-critical core; built & parallel-run before going live.

2 · Foreign Worker Lifecycle & Dormitory

FW · Assistant

Worker database, work-permit issue/renew/cancel workflow, passport & medical-exam scheduling, status/AWOL tracking, home-leave management & holds, dormitory bed forecast & allocation, OFWAS/PCP, repatriation, MOM upgrading (CSOC/CoreTrade, R2→R1).

3 · Staff Lifecycle

Staff · Assistant

Onboarding / confirmation / exit workflows, staff directory, org-chart maintenance & publication, training-bond register, attrition tracking & exit interviews.

4 · Case Management — Grievance & Discipline

Manager · FW · Staff

Disciplinary workflow (incident → fact-finding → warning → suspension → termination review), grievance escalation chain, MC-abuse monitoring — with full audit trail and DOA routing.

5 · Claims & Work Injury

FW · Staff · Payroll · Manager

WICA / FWMI / Group Medical claim cases, MOM injury notifications (10-day timelines), AME data, settlement packages, DOA routing. Cross-functional.

6 · Levy & Waiver

FW · Payroll

Waiver trigger identification (FW) → application (Payroll) → reconciliation vs MOM billing; levy billing & R1/R2 cost differences.

7 · Reporting & Analytics

all roles

Consolidated manpower-cost, AWOL, attrition, OT-exception, status-breakdown and headcount-reconciliation reports (the HR Assurance Summary already lives on the dashboard).

Tier 2 · cycles & enablingPhase 2 / 3

8 · Performance Management

Staff · FW · Manager

Quarterly staff & worker review cycles, performance / attendance / EHS records, increment & bonus generation tied to reviews, appeals.

9 · Compensation & Benefits

Manager · Payroll

Annual salary & bonus review cycle (data → equity → consolidated proposal → envelope approval), market benchmarking, insurance renewal & policy records.

10 · Grants & Training

Manager · FW · Staff

Government grants / wage subsidies / capability funding, training grants & levy rebates, training coordination (CSOC/CoreTrade, upskilling), training-needs tracking.

11 · BCA Licence & Tender Documentation

Manager · Staff

Compile HR doc packages for A1 renewals & tender submissions (headcount schedules, trade-cert registers), manpower sufficiency, cross-check vs Operations deployment.

12 · Admin & Onboarding Logistics

HR Assistant

The Assistant's workspace: onboarding kits, asset issuance/recovery, document scanning & OCR indexing, HRIQ account creation, master trackers, repatriation logistics.

Tier 3 · governance & extrasPhase 3

13 · Data Governance & Admin Console

Manager

RBAC administration, retention enforcement, breach reporting, AI-tool approval register, audit-log viewer, HRIQ sync & data-freshness status, cross-training matrix.

14 · Policy & Communications

Manager · Staff

Policy lifecycle & version control, memo / announcement approval & distribution (drafting already exists in Documents).

15 · Welfare & Engagement

FW · Staff

Worker welfare & festive programmes, staff engagement initiatives, event logistics.

04 / NOT GAPS

Human-only tasks — supported, not automated

These stay human by design (judgment, legal accountability, relationships, physical acts). The modules above support them; they don't need a module to "do" them — so their absence isn't a gap.

  • Fronting MOM inspections & audits · final disciplinary & grievance adjudication · salary/bonus appeal decisions
  • Interviews & exit interviews · team leadership & mentorship · relationship-building with agencies / partners
  • Physical dormitory check-ins, asset issuance/recovery, ticketing & transport · cross-training coverage in person
Assessed against the task inventory in HR JOBS.docx / the HR Portal Blueprint. Status is a planning judgement of the current prototype, not a code audit. Counts are approximate; several task groups span more than one module. Priorities align with the phasing in the Requirements Specification.
