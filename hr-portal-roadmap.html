02 / PHASES

What each phase delivers

Phase 0 · Foundation

~6–10 wksNothing user-facing — the rails everything else runs on.
  • SG-region cloud, private networking, secrets vault, CI/CD, 3 environments, backups.
  • SSO (Entra ID) + MFA, RBAC for the six roles, immutable audit log.
  • PostgreSQL schema + HRIQ ingestion pipeline (productionise your Python-to-Excel scripts: schedule, validate, load).
  • AI gateway (de-identification, logging, model integration).
Exit gate: a user can log in, see role-scoped data synced from HRIQ, and every action is audit-logged.

Phase 1 · Value modules

~10–14 wksTurn the six prototype modules into the real thing. No money touched.
  • Role dashboards · Compliance Monitor (real rules engine + alerts) · Approvals/workflow engine.
  • Document Generator (real DOCX merge on uploaded letterhead templates, sandbox, history).
  • Knowledge Assistant (RAG over curated EA/EFMA/MOM/SOP knowledge base).
  • Recruitment (CV → object store → AI parse → fit-score → pipeline → emails → employment letter).
Exit gate: HR uses the portal daily for monitoring, drafting, knowledge & hiring. Independent security review + pen test here before anything handles more sensitive data.

Phase 2 · Core operations

~16–22 wksThe operational backbone — including the money-critical payroll engine.
  • Payroll & Cost Control · Levy & Waiver · FW Lifecycle & Dormitory · Staff Lifecycle.
  • Case Management (grievance/discipline) · Claims & Work Injury · Reporting & Analytics.
Exit gate: payroll runs in parallel with the current process and reconciles clean for 2–3 cycles before it becomes authoritative. Statutory submissions remain human-released.

Phase 3 · Cycles, enabling & governance

~12–16 wksComplete the picture.
  • Performance Management · Compensation & Benefits · Grants & Training · Licence & Tender Docs.
  • Admin & Onboarding Logistics (Assistant workspace) · Data Governance & Admin Console · Policy & Comms · Welfare.
Exit gate: full task coverage; HRIQ becomes a back-office system of record with the portal as the working surface.

04 / TEAM & CAVEATS

Team, ways of working & caveats

Team

  • 1–2 full-stack engineers (Python/FastAPI + React), AI-assisted.
  • You as product owner + compliance sign-off.
  • A part-time security reviewer for the pre-Phase-2 gate.
  • An HR/payroll SME to validate calculations during parallel-run.

Ways of working

  • Ship a usable slice each phase — value lands early (Phase 1), not at the end.
  • Automated tests mandatory for all payroll/levy calculations.
  • Human-in-the-loop release gates stay throughout.
  • No real personal data in Dev; masked data in Staging.

What could move the numbers

  • HRIQ access — if batch export is awkward, ingestion grows.
  • Payroll complexity — many pay components/edge cases add weeks.
  • Regulatory verification — rules must be confirmed, not assumed.
  • Scope creep — each "small extra" per module compounds.
Recommended first moveDon't commit to the whole programme up front. Fund Phase 0 + Phase 1 first (~16–24 eng-wks / ~4–6 months for a small team). That delivers a genuinely useful HR-only portal — monitoring, drafting, knowledge, hiring — proves the architecture and the team, and lets you decide on Phase 2 with real evidence rather than a paper plan.
Estimates are rough order-of-magnitude for planning, not a fixed quote — they depend on final scope, HRIQ access, team experience and regulatory verification.
