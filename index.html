WHERE THINGS STAND

Status & next steps

Done: analysis, requirements, architecture, a working Phase-1 prototype, gap analysis and a costed roadmap. Decisions made: HR-only access, human-in-the-loop on consequential actions, deterministic code for payroll/statutory math, PDPA-by-design, MVP-first phasing, and a cost-aware HRIQ batch sync (reusing your Python-to-Excel extraction).

Before any build

Confirm the HRIQ extraction approach and have a compliance person verify the statutory rules.

Recommended first move

Fund Phase 0 + Phase 1 (~4–6 months, small team) — a usable portal that proves the architecture before the money-critical payroll work.

Open options

Refine the prototype further, prototype a Phase-2 module (e.g. Payroll or FW Lifecycle), or confirm hosting & identity provider.
