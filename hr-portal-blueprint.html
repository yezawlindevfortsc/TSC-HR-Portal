00 / HOW TO READ THIS

The three AI modes

Every task below carries one of three tags. The tag tells you who does the work and who is accountable — the portal is designed around this distinction, not around replacing people.

🤖 AI-led

AI does the task end-to-end — calculation, monitoring, drafting, reconciliation, collation. A human triggers it and glances at the output. Highest automation value.

🤝 AI-assisted

AI prepares the heavy lifting — a draft, an analysis, a recommendation, a flagged exception — but a named person reviews and makes the call. Human stays accountable.

👤 Human-only

Judgment, legal accountability, relationships, physical acts, and final authority. AI may surface information, but the act itself must be performed by a person.

01 / DEPARTMENT

The six roles

The Head of HR sits above the team as final approver and escalation point for strategic and high-risk matters (no operational task list of their own). The five operating roles below carry the day-to-day work. Operational execution flows upward through defined approval thresholds (the DOA matrix).

HR Manager

Governance, Risk & Strategy

Reports to the Head of HR. Strategic partner and central authority for compliance, risk and compensation. Approves and adjudicates at department level, routes higher-impact matters up. Mostly an approval & judgment role — so AI mostly assists here rather than leads.

03 / THE HUMAN LINE

Tasks that stay human

These are fine to keep manual — they require judgment, legal accountability, a physical act, or a human relationship. AI may surface information into them, but should never perform them autonomously. Build the portal to support these, not replace them.

04 / THE BUILD

How the portal could be structured

An HR-only application: every staff member logs into a role-shaped dashboard surfacing their tasks and the AI tools that serve them. No general-employee access. A shared layer of AI services powers all five workspaces.

🔒 Access & data layer — the non-negotiables

  • HR-only authentication. Corporate SSO restricted to the HR security group. The portal is not visible to other employees — separate entirely from any future employee self-service.
  • Role-based access control (RBAC) mirroring the six roles. A Payroll Executive sees payroll & cost tools; an FW Executive sees worker-lifecycle tools; the Manager sees everything plus approvals. Access levels approved by the HR Manager under PDPA.
  • PDPA-by-design. Role-scoped data visibility, full audit logging of every AI action, automatic enforcement of the Employment Act retention clock, and breach-alert protocols.
  • Maker-checker & DOA routing built in. AI prepares, a named human reviews/approves, and every approval is logged against the Delegation of Authority matrix.
  • Human-in-the-loop by default. AI never finalises payroll, sends a statutory submission, or issues a letter without a human release step.

Shared AI services

Seven engines reused across all roles — this is where most of the 44 AI-led tasks live.

Compliance & Early-Warning Monitor cross-role

The single most valuable engine for a BCA A1 contractor — continuous watch over every regulatory clock, alerting before a breach.

  • DRC headroom, BCA quota, IPA pipeline vs quota, R1/R2 ratios
  • Work-permit / passport / medical-exam expiry; licence conditions & financial grading
  • OT statutory caps (Part IV), FCF 14-day advertising window, record-retention purge dates

Calculation & Reconciliation Engine Payroll · FW · Staff

Deterministic math with anomaly detection — payroll, levies, encashment, bond-break, AME/compensation, premium benchmarking, manpower-cost reports. Runs under maker-checker.

Document & Letter Generator cross-role

Drafts increment/bonus/bond/warning letters, JDs, memos, announcements, and MOM notification drafts from templates + HRIQ data. Always routed to a human for approval.

HR Knowledge Assistant cross-role

Retrieval-grounded Q&A over the Employment Act, EFMA, MOM circulars, BCA conditions, internal SOPs and the DOA matrix. Answers "can we…?" with a cited source — never freehand legal advice.

Report Builder Manager · all

One-click generation of the weekly HR Assurance Summary, manpower cost by cost centre, OT exception, AWOL, attrition and status-breakdown reports.

Workflow, Approvals & Case Tracking cross-role

Routes maker-checker and DOA approvals, tracks grievance/disciplinary cases through their defined flow, manages escalation chains, and keeps a complete audit trail.

Recruitment & Intake Assistant Staff · Assistant

Resume parsing, shortlisting, interview scheduling, agency-performance tracking, and triage/routing of incoming HR inquiries and walk-in applications.

Role workspaces

Each login lands on a dashboard composed from the shared services, filtered to that role's tasks.

HR Manager console

Approval queue, HR Assurance Summary, compliance risk board, comp-review workspace, team workload view, PDPA & AI-governance controls.

Payroll workspace

Payroll run + reconciliation, CPF/IR21/IR8A prep, levy & waiver processing, OT audit, cost-centre reporting, insurance records.

Foreign-Worker workspace

Live DRC/IPA board, permit lifecycle, dormitory forecast, worker status & AWOL tracking, home-leave management, claims compilation.

Staff workspace

Recruitment pipeline + FCF tracker, onboarding/exit workflows, org chart & directory, performance cycles, attrition analytics, staff claims.

Assistant workspace

Master trackers, document scanning/indexing, OT follow-up queue, account-creation tasks, expiry-alert dashboard, inquiry routing.
